What is Modular High-Rise Construction?

Modular high-rise construction involves building pre-fabricated, prefinished volumetric units off-site in a controlled factory environment. These units are then transported and assembled on-site to create high-rise buildings. This method offers greater efficiency, faster construction times, and improved quality control.

What are the benefits of Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction (PPVC)?

PPVC allows for faster project completion, reduced labor costs, improved safety standards, and a more sustainable approach to construction by reducing material waste. It also ensures higher precision and quality as the modules are manufactured in a controlled environment.

What types of buildings can you construct using modular construction?

We specialize in constructing high-rise residential, commercial, and mixed-use buildings. Our modular construction solutions are adaptable to various building types, ensuring flexibility in design and functionality.

What certifications and accreditations does Moderna Homes have?

Moderna Homes is accredited under the Building and Construction Authority’s (BCA) Manufacturer Accreditation Scheme (MAS) for our factories in Malaysia and Singapore. Our PPVC design system is also approved by Hong Kong’s Building Department's IPA scheme.
