WHO WE ARE

Moderna Homes Pte Ltd is one of the pioneer and the specialist in modular high-rise construction in Singapore since 2013. Our sterling track record in Prefabricated Prefinished Volumetric Construction (PPVC) has positioned us as one of the top-of-mind choice in the industry

We are wholly owned subsidiary of BBR Holdings Ltd, a public listed company on Singapore Stock Exchange, Moderna Homes is a licensed specialist builder for both steel and concrete PPVC. Our factories in Malaysia and Singapore are accredited by BCA’s Manufacturer Accreditation Scheme (MAS) in year 2017. Moderna Homes PPVC design system has also been approved by Hong Kong’s Building Department’s IPA scheme in April 2019.
